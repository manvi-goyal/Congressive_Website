Congressive

Congress at Your Fingertips

Find Out More

It's in the App Store!

Congressive is an application which enables users to contact their officials in Congress. Users can look up their home locations to more easily access information about their elected officials, including each elected official's top campaign contributors.

This application is not for profit, and is powered by the Sunlight Foundation's Congress and Influence Explorer APIs.
